About Me

Hi, I’m Amin, a Master of Management student based in Wellington. I’m all about combining curiosity, creativity, and a drive to make a difference—whether it’s in business, research, or helping others.

Right now, I work as an R&D Specialist at Market Researcher, while completing my Master of Management degree in Wellington. My work revolves around market analysis, assisting businesses uncover new opportunities, and turning ideas into actionable plans.

When I’m not working, I volunteer with Red Cross New Zealand, supporting vulnerable communities in Kāpiti coast. I’m also a researcher in the field of energy science, with publications focused on addressing energy-related hardships faced by vulnerable communities.

My hobbies are hitting the gym and studying Japanese to stay fluent.

And in my free time? I love learning new things! like building this website from scratch with absolutely no experience (and having fun with new challenges!).
