About Me

Hi, I’m Amin, a Master of Management student based in Wellington. I’m all about combining curiosity, creativity, and a drive to make a difference—whether it’s in business, research, or helping others.

Right now, I work as a Research Assistant at MarketResearcher.ai, while completing my Master of Management degree in Wellington. My work revolves around market analysis, assisting businesses uncover opportunities, and turning ideas into actionable plans.

My hobbies are hitting the gym and studying Japanese to stay fluent.

And in my free time? I love learning new things—like building this website from scratch with absolutely no experience (and having fun along the way!).
